About Us

Chatsworth Jumping Castle Hire

Since 1999, Chatsworth Jumping Castle Hire has been creating cherished memories for families in our community. We were born from a love of bringing people together — making every celebration a little brighter and every laugh a little louder.

We pour our hearts into what we do, ensuring each inflatable is sparkling clean, meticulously cared for, and ready to fill your day with endless fun. Our team treats every event as if it were our own, providing warm, personalized service to make your special moments truly unforgettable.

From birthday parties to community gatherings, we're here to help you create magical experiences that will be remembered for years to come.
